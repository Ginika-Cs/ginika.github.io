Knowledge Base Samples

Sample FAQ Responses

Examples of help centre content I've written, clear, accurate, and written from the user's perspective.

QWhy hasn't my deposit reflected in my wallet?
Deposits typically reflect within 5–15 minutes, but can take up to 2 hours during peak periods. If your deposit hasn't appeared after 2 hours, check that: (1) the payment was not declined by your bank, (2) you used the correct account details, and (3) the minimum deposit amount was met. If the issue persists, contact support with your bank's transaction reference number and we'll investigate immediately.
QHow do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Account Settings → Billing → Cancel Plan. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. You won't be charged again after cancellation. Note: cancelling does not automatically trigger a refund for the current period. If you believe you were charged in error, please contact our support team within 48 hours of the charge.
QMy account is locked. How do I regain access?
Accounts are locked after 5 failed login attempts as a security measure. To unlock: (1) Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, (2) Check your inbox, including spam, for a reset link, (3) Use the link within 30 minutes as it expires. If you no longer have access to your registered email, contact support with a government-issued ID to verify your identity and we'll update your email address manually.
QI was charged but didn't make a purchase. What should I do?
If you see an unexpected charge, do not panic. First check whether it's a subscription renewal, a pending transaction, or a payment attempt that may have posted late. If none of these apply, contact support immediately with: your account email, the charge amount, and the date it appeared. We take unauthorised charges seriously and will investigate within 24 hours. If confirmed as an error, a full refund will be processed within 3–5 business days.
QHow do I update my payment method?
Go to Account Settings → Billing → Payment Methods and click "Add New Card." Enter your card details and set it as the default payment method. Your old card will be removed automatically on your next billing cycle once the new card is confirmed. If you're having trouble adding a card, ensure your bank has not blocked international online transactions. Contact your bank to enable this if needed.
QCan I use the platform in multiple countries?
Yes, the platform is accessible globally. However, certain payment methods may vary by country. If you're travelling or relocating, your account and data remain intact. Note that if your IP address is flagged by our security system as unusual, you may be asked to verify your identity via a one-time code sent to your registered email or phone. This is a standard security measure to protect your account.
